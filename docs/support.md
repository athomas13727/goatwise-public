## Common Questions

### Does GoatWise require an internet connection?

No. GoatWise is designed to work offline.

### Does GoatWise collect personal information?

No. GoatWise does not use accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, or backend servers. Quiz progress is stored locally on the device.

### How do I reset scores?

Open the Settings tab in GoatWise and use the reset scores option.

### How do I turn sound effects on or off?

Use the sound toggle in the app.

# GoatWise Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

GoatWise is an offline goat skill-a-thon practice app for kids and teens. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

## Summary

GoatWise does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not use:

- User accounts or sign in
- Advertising
- Analytics
- Tracking
- In-app purchases
- Location services
- Camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or other sensitive device permissions
- Network requests or backend servers

## Information Stored On Device

GoatWise stores quiz progress locally on the user's device so the app can show practice scores for today and this week. This may include quiz attempts, whether answers were correct, the related topic and difficulty, and the date of the attempt.

GoatWise also stores the sound effects preference locally on the user's device.

This information stays on the device and is not sent to GoatWise, the developer, or any third party.

## Children's Privacy

GoatWise is designed for youth goat skill-a-thon practice. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or teens. Because the app does not use accounts, tracking, analytics, ads, or network services, no personal information is requested from young users.

## Data Sharing

GoatWise does not share user data with third parties.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Quiz progress remains on the device until the user resets scores in the app, deletes the app, or clears the app's stored data through iOS.

## Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated if GoatWise adds features that affect privacy. Any future changes will be posted in the public GoatWise docs repository.
